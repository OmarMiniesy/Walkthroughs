
#### Web site defacement

Today is Alice's first day at the Wayne Enterprises' Security Operations Center. Lucius sits Alice down and gives her first assignment: A memo from Gotham City Police Department (GCPD). Apparently GCPD has found evidence online ([http://pastebin.com/Gw6dWjS9](http://pastebin.com/Gw6dWjS9)) that the website [www.imreallynotbatman.com](http://www.imreallynotbatman.com/) hosted on Wayne Enterprises' IP address space has been compromised. The group has multiple objectives... but a key aspect of their modus operandi is to deface websites in order to embarrass their victim. Lucius has asked Alice to determine if [www.imreallynotbatman.com](http://www.imreallynotbatman.com/). (the personal blog of Wayne Corporations CEO) was really compromised.
